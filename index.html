Who We Are

Work that
Matters.

Thought Projector Studios is a strategic planning consultancy for people and organizations doing work that matters. We partner with values-led businesses, creatives, cultural organizations, educators, advocates, and nonprofits to bring structure to big ideas and help turn them into action.

Our work is grounded in respect and a belief that strategy should support both the mission and the people behind it.
